Aaron Carter Gets Forehead Tattoo Of His Girlfriends Name Check It Out!

Aaron Carter took to social media to show off his new ink and it turns out that not only is it a face tattoo, it’s also dedicated to his girlfriend of three months, Melanie Martin! For most people, this is a double ‘no-no’ but the singer seems, sure enough, their love is forever already to make this permanent change!

That being said, the word ‘Melanie’ is now being proudly displayed on Aaron’s forehead.

That is some serious commitment!

But that was not the only ink he got recently as there are also three crescent moons across his cheekbone just below the name written in cursive font above his eyebrow.

The snaps featuring the tattoos were actually intended to show off his new hair color and not the ink but, of course, social media immediately noticed the change.

Hairstylist Brandon Peach shared a snap and alongside it, he wrote: ‘Aaron Carter. Had so much fun hanging out with the guy and bringing back his signature bright blonde.’

Hopefully, Aaron and Melanie will last for a long time and he will never regret the name tattoo!

This is not the only big step the singer took, however!

As you may be aware, after the tattoo session, the pair went diamond rings shopping and sure enough, a clip was shared on social media in which Melanie tries on a huge engagement diamond at a jeweler’s.

Aaron can be heard asking about the price of the first diamond ring and the answer which is ‘$80k’ does not seem to faze him.
