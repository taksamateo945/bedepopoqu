How Many Kids Does The Coach Have?

Jim Harbaugh’s kids are seven children from his 2 different marriages. His children are Jay Harbaugh, Jack Harbaugh, Addison Harbaugh, Katherine Harbaugh, Grace Harbaugh, John Harbaugh, and James Harbaugh.

The Michigan Wolverines head coach has been married twice. His first marriage was to Miah Harbaugh and it lasted from 1996 to 2006. The ten-year union produced three children – Jay Harbaugh, Grace Harbaugh, and James Harbaugh. Sarah Feuerborn Harbaugh is the second woman Jim married and they tied the knot in 2008. The two are still together to date and welcomed four children – daughters Addison and Katherine, and sons Jack and John.

Meet Jim Harbaugh’s Children

Jay Harbaugh is Jim’s First Child

  • Date of Birth: June 14, 1989
  • Age: 34 Years Old

Born June 14, 1989, Jay Harbaugh is Jim Harbaugh and Miah Harbaugh’s first child together. Jay was born in San Diego, California, and his date of birth indicates that he was born a few years before his parents got married.

Jay Harbaugh is a graduate of St. Augustine High School where he played defensive line and would have launched an active football career if a knee injury didn’t end his playing days. He furthered his education at Oregon State University, earning a degree in Sociology.

Jay is currently an American football coach and his records are spread across different teams. He first worked as an undergraduate assistant at Oregon State for four seasons and spent three seasons under his uncle John Harbaugh at the Ravens. In 2015, he served as the tight ends coach of the Michigan football team and assistant special teams coach for the Wolverines. He has remained with the team to date.

Jim Harbaugh’s first son is now married and has two children.

Does Jim Harbaugh Have Twin Children?

There are several speculations suggesting that Jim has twins in his home, but this is not true. All his seven children were born separately by their moms. Jim Harbaugh’s children were born in separate years, and none came as twins.

Another twin rumor that has followed Jim is that he and his brother John are twins, but this is also not true. In fact, John Harbaugh is the older brother of Jim Harbough. While John was born on 23rd September 1962, Jim was born on 23rd December 1963. Due to the closeness of their birth, people tend to think they are twins, but they are not.

Both Harbaugh brothers are doing well in their careers as football coaches. Like Jim, John is also an American football coach, and he is currently serving as the head coach of the Baltimore Ravens of the National Football League (NFL). Since he joined the Raven in 2008, John has led the team to 145 wins.

Is Jim Harbaugh Currently Married?

Jim Harbaugh is presently married to Sarah Feuerborn Harbaugh. They tied the knot in 2008, which is two years after Jim divorced his first wife, Miah Harbaugh. Sarah is a native of Delton, Missouri, and is a charity worker.

Who are the Mothers of Jim Harbaugh’s Children?

The mothers of Jim Harbaugh’s children are his two spouses, Miah and Sarah. His ex-wife Miah gave birth to his first three children Jay, James, and Grace while his second and current wife, Sarah, gave birth to four of his children namely Jack, Addison, Katherine, and John.

Who is Jim Harbaugh?

Jim Harbaugh is an American college football coach who is the present coach of the University of Michigan football team, the Michigan Wolverines. Jim Harbaugh has won some accolades in his career, including AP College Football Coach of the Year for 2021.
