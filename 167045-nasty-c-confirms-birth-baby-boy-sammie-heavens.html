Award-winning rapper David Junior Ngcobo, popularly known as Nasty C, has added the role of being a father to his list of accolades.

Nasty C welcomes a healthy baby boy

The rapper whispered in ZiMoja's ears that he received his new bundle of joy a few weeks ago with his high school sweetheart, Sammie Heavens:

"He arrived a few weeks ago. He is a strong little boy. I get to go home to so much joy. I'm loving every minute of it. I even gained some daddy fat," said the rapper.

Nasty C dedicates Dear Oliver to his son

Nasty C had people excited and curious when he teased with his new song Dear Oliver. His online fans were sure that would possibly be his baby's name, but it is now evident that the bundle had already arrived.

He says they have given Oliver three names, an isiZulu, English and Japanese name.

He also confirmed that Dear Oliver will detail the journey of his new role as a dad.
