&quot;No Words&quot;Tyler Perry Breaks His Silence After Bobbi Kristina Browns Death

Just days after Bobbi Kristina Brown‘s death at age 22, the late Whitney Houston‘s friend Tyler Perry is speaking out about the young woman’s tragic passing.

“I’m a writer with no words…. Bobbi Kris,” the 45-year-old actor wrote on Twitter on Tuesday, July 28.

Tyler has been lending his support to Bobbi Kristina’s father, Bobby Brown, 46, and his family since the R&B singer’s daughter was placed in a medically-induced coma after she was found unconscious in a bathtub inside her Roswell, Ga. home in January.

Nearly two weeks ago, the I Can Do Bad All by Myself star took to his Facebook page to ask fans to “pray for Krissi, and the Houston and Brown families,” during this difficult time.

“This morning I woke up with Whitney on my mind. I couldn’t help but think about all that she must have dreamed for her child. I know this was not her dream,” he began the emotional post.

Since Bobbi Kristina’s passing, Tyler reportedly traveled to Atlanta to be with the Brown and Houston families and help plan funeral arrangements.
