Where are the Lizzie McGuire Movie cast now?

13 years ago The Lizzie McGuire Movie was released! Lizzie went off on a dream vacation around Rome and bumped into the deceivingly beautiful pop sensation Paolo. We have put together a list of where the cast from this iconic movie is now…

Hilary Duff

The wonderful character Lizzie is what made both the movie and show so lovable! Hilary has an adorable son now. As well as starting a little family, she released her fifth album Breathe In. Breathe Out. and starred in the movie Flock of Kids. She has definitely kept herself busy by being a lead in the  television series Younger.

Adam Lamberg

We all fell in love with the sweet and caring Gordo all those years ago (still now over that Lizzie and Gordo got together!!). Adam is not active on social media and is no longer acting. The last we have heard from him is

Jake Thomas

Jake’s character Matt was the epitome of “annoying little brother”! He has filmed several television movies along with the web series Storytellers. Make sure you check out his Instagram to catch a glimpse of his beautiful photography!

Hallie Todd

Lizzie’s mom, Jo was certainly a lovable character, who crazily agreed to fly all the way to Rome to see Lizzie! Hallie’s current projects include producing and starring in The Last Champion. Her also shares her years of experience in the business with her acting classes.

Sam was not entirely sure of what his kids were doing half of the time, but we still love him nonetheless. After taking a break from acting, Robert is currently filming for the movie Justice. No other projects have been announced yet.

We saw a new side to Kate in the movie, she was actually nice to Lizzie!! We honestly thought the day would never come. Ashlie has a beautiful daughter she occasionally posts pictures of  her on Instagram, along with throwback Lizzie McGuire pictures! She stopped acting after The Lizzie McGuire Movie.

Clayton played the ditsy bad boy everyone wanted to be with! Over the past three years, he has become active in his acting career again. Starring in many short movies and television series, with his most recent role being in Still in San Pedro .

Yani Gellman

This one might be odd to hear for Pretty Little Liars fans, but Yani, who played Paolo is Officer Garrett Reynolds!! Paolo certainly had us fooled with his charm when he first bumped into Lizzie.  Now, Yani has continued to appear in television movies and series over the past few years.

Miss Ungermeyer had so many hilarious quotes and the best romance with Sergei! Alex has an endless list to her filmography career so far! Most recently known for Shameless, Bordertown, and voicing Lois Griffin in Family Guy.
