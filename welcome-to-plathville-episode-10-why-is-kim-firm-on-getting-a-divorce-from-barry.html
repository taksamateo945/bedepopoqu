Welcome to Plathville Episode 10: Why is Kim firm on getting a divorce from Barry?

Tuesday night saw the return of a brand-new, harrowing episode of TLC’s Welcome to Plathville. Kim made the decision to speak with Barry this week and let him know that she truly wants to move forward with the divorce.

The Plath family has endured numerous changes since the season began, particularly Kim and Barry. Barry admitted that he still loved his wife even if Kim had her own reasons for wanting to dissolve their marriage. Sadly, that didn’t satisfy Kim’s desire to leave.

Barry started off Welcome to Plathville episode 10 by assisting Kim in mounting a bed above her dancing studio. Kim revealed that the space would be rented out for travel. She revealed about their marriage that it resembled a business partnership and added that she was adamant about the divorce and didn’t want to patch things up.
