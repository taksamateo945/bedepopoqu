Christina Aguilera is more than just glowing in the latest issue of V magazine. The pregnant singer—expecting her second child, her first with fiance Matt Rutler—stripped down and went completely nude for an ultra-sexy photo shoot, showing off her curves and growing baby bump.

In one black and white shot, the Voice mentor posed with her head back while covering her breasts with her blonde tousled waves and right hand. In a second, she wore a see-through white dress, which exposed her chest.
